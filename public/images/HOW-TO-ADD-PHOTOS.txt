HOW TO ADD YOUR REAL iSHOT CONCEPT PHOTOS
==========================================

1. Copy your photo files into THIS folder (public/images/).
   Use simple lowercase names, no spaces. Examples:
     hero.jpg
     wedding-1.jpg
     studio-portrait.jpg

2. Open  src/assets.js  and replace the placeholder link with your file.
   A file named  hero.jpg  in this folder is written as  "/images/hero.jpg"

   Before:  homeHero: "https://picsum.photos/seed/ishotherowedding/1800/1100",
   After:   homeHero: "/images/hero.jpg",

3. Save. The site updates automatically while "npm run dev" is running.

LOGO
====
Put your logo file in the  public/  folder (one level up), e.g.  public/logo.svg
Then in  src/assets.js  set:   src: "/logo.svg"
Use a WHITE or TRANSPARENT logo — it sits on the dark navy header and footer.

TIPS
====
- Good formats: .jpg (photos), .png or .svg (logo/graphics), .webp (smallest).
- Keep photos reasonably sized (long edge ~1600-2000px) so the site stays fast.
- You can name files anything; just match the name in src/assets.js.
